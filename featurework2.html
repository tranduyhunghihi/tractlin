"Cho Dong Qua Ngot" was born with the desire to bring an interesting perspective on the topic of "tropical fruits" in Vietnam.
This is not a purely theoratical handbook, but a story told with pictures and colors by young people who have lived, experienced and withnessed the daily activities of Vietnamese people, recognizing the beauty in traditional markets associated with their daily lives.
Combining it with infomation about some familiar tropical fruits to create a fun and spontaneous combination so that readers can have a fun, nostalgic and also very useful experience.
